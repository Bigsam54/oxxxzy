# Oxzy Platform – MVP Website

## 🚀 Overview

This project is the official MVP website for **Oxzy**, a youth-driven innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem focused on empowering young people across Ghana.

The platform is designed to serve as the digital foundation for Oxzy's core initiatives, including:

* Young Entrepreneurs Summit (YES)
* Young Entrepreneurs Club
* Ghana Youth Government

This MVP focuses on building a strong digital presence, onboarding users, and collecting structured data to support future growth and platform development.

---

## 🎯 Product Goal

The primary goal of this website is to:

* Onboard young entrepreneurs and innovators
* Drive registrations for Oxzy programs
* Collect meaningful user data for future engagement
* Establish Oxzy as a credible and scalable ecosystem

---

## 🧠 Product Thinking

This is not just a website — it is designed as a **platform-first system**.

Key principles:

* Conversion-focused (signup-driven)
* Mobile-first (target audience: young Ghanaians)
* Scalable structure for future product expansion
* Data-driven foundation for decision making

---

## 🏗️ Structure

The website is organized into the following core sections:

### 1. Homepage

* Introduces Oxzy's vision
* Highlights key programs
* Drives primary actions (Join / Register)

### 2. Programs

* Overview of all Oxzy initiatives

### 3. Young Entrepreneurs Summit (YES)

* Event information
* Registration interface

### 4. Entrepreneurs Club

* Core community onboarding system
* Primary data collection point

### 5. Ghana Youth Government

* Policy and innovation initiative
* Participation entry point

### 6. Contact

* Communication and inquiries

---

## 🎨 Design System

The design is inspired by Ghana's national identity and youth culture:

* Red – Energy & urgency
* Gold – Opportunity & optimism
* Green – Growth & innovation
* Black – Strength & authority

Typography and layout follow a clean, modern startup aesthetic with a focus on clarity and usability.

---

## 📊 Data Collection Strategy

The platform is intentionally structured to collect meaningful user data, including:

* Demographics (age, location)
* Interests and industries
* Skill levels
* Entrepreneurial intent

This data will be used to:

* Improve program design
* Personalize user experiences
* Guide future product development
